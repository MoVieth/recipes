Ingredients for the yellow sweet potato curry with broccoli, leek and fragrant rice :
4 pax

- 2 large sweet potatoes
- 250 g broccoli
- 1 stick of leek
- 1 stick of celery
- 100 g carrot
- 100 g onion
- 1 bunch of coriander
- 1 handful Thai basil
- 50 g fresh ginger
- 1-2 teaspoons curry
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- Some sesame oil
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce
- Juice of one lemon
- salt / sugar
- Scented rice
- 2 pieces star anise
- 2 pieces bay leaves
- Some sweet chili sauce


Preparation:

1 Peel the sweet potato, onion and carrot. Cut the sweet potato and the onion into fine cubes, the carrot into thin strips. Wash the broccoli. Wash the leek and the celery and cut diagonally into strips. Peel and chop the ginger.

Saut√© 2 sweet potatoes and onion in some sesame oil in a wok. Deglaze with water so that the potatoes and onions are completely covered. Simmer for about 30 to 40 minutes until the sweet potatoes are so soft that they can be crushed with a wooden spoon. Add ginger, curry, soy sauce and 1-2 tbsp. sesame oil and finally season to taste with salt, sugar and a little sweet chili sauce.

3 Place one cup of fragrant rice with two cups of water, star anise, bay leaves in a pot, bring to the boil, stir once, salt, reduce heat drastically, cover with a lid and simmer for 15 minutes until the water has disappeared.

4 When the rice is ready, add the carrots, leek, celery and broccoli to the curry sauce and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring. Season to taste with lemon juice and serve with the herbs.
